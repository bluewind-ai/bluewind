# Bluewind for agencies

Bluewind is not a copyleft open source license. This means you can resell or redistribute the core functionalities of Bluewind to any partner or customer, without having to open source your code in return.

Similar to GHL, you can resell Bluewind to your customers. You can even modify the source code and add all the improvements you want.

Bluewind is built to be ubiquitous. You can invest your engineering resources into this CRM knowing that the technologies being used will always be relevant for your organization.

Bluewind is completely self-hostable and enterprise ready.
